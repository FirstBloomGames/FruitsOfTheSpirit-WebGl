Privacy Policy

Fruit of the Spirit: Orchard Bloom
Effective July 22, 2026

First Bloom Games designed this peaceful Scripture match game to work without an account and without asking players for personal information.

Information stored by the game

Game progress, settings, scores, tree growth, and unlocked Scripture cards are stored locally on the player's device or browser. First Bloom Games does not receive that local save data.

Information we do not collect

The current game does not ask for names, email addresses, contacts, precise location, photos, microphone access, or account credentials. It does not include third-party analytics, behavioral advertising, or in-game purchases.

Web hosting and app stores

Web hosts, device platforms, and app stores may process standard connection, device, download, crash, or purchase information under their own privacy policies. First Bloom Games does not control those independent services.

Children's privacy

The game does not knowingly collect personal information from children. A parent or guardian may contact us with any privacy concern.

Updates and contact

If future versions add optional ads, purchases, analytics, accounts, or online services, this policy will be updated before those features are released.
