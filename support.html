Quick help

  • Tap or swipe one fruit toward a neighboring fruit to make a match.
  • Music and sound effects have separate controls in Settings.
  • The complete Love Tree is free. Tapping another locked tree explains the optional one-time Full Orchard unlock.
  • Unlocked verses, scores, tree growth, and preferences are saved on this device.
  • Because no game account is required, gameplay progress does not currently transfer between devices and can be removed when the app or browser data is deleted.

Purchases and restoration

Full Orchard is a permanent non-consumable purchase. In the installed mobile app, open Settings, choose Full Orchard, then choose Restore Purchases while signed into the same Apple or Google store account. Reset Progress does not remove Full Orchard ownership.

Report a problem

Please include the device model, operating-system version, app version, what you expected, and the steps that caused the problem. A screenshot is welcome when it does not contain private information.

Privacy

The game does not require an account or ask for personal information. Read the public privacy policy for full details.
